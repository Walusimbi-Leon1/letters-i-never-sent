Letters I Never Sent

A Love Novel

By Walusimbi Leon (SGSS)

About

Letters I Never Sent is a love story about the words we leave unspoken, the love letters we never mail, and the second chances that arrive when we least expect them. A story about the architecture of absence and the courage it takes to speak the words that live in the spaces between silence.

International Edition

A journalist in New York finds a box of 47 unsent love letters in a Greenwich Village bookshop. The trail leads her to Paris, where she meets the woman the letters were written for — and discovers that the most important stories are the ones we never tell.
